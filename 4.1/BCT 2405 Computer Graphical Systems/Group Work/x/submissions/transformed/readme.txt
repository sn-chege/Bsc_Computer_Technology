The question:
Scale and anotate 60 degrees clockwise

The solution:
The 3 folders contain different attempts on the question
1. rotated - This is the result on applying a rotation of 60 degrees
2. scaled - This is the result after scaling by 0.5 and a translation of (50,50)
3. scaled and rotated - This is the result after scaling by 0.5, a rotation of 60 degrees and a translation of (-500,500).
